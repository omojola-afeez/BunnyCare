Who We Are

A Legacy of Compassionate Elder Care

BunnyCare Care Services is a trusted provider of quality elderly care in Oyo State, Nigeria. Our beautiful dementia-friendly care home — Hope Villa — is nestled in Felele Rab, Ibadan, offering 10 fully furnished bedrooms with all the comforts of home, including a gazebo and a mini-farm with chickens, rabbits, ducks and goats.

Our senior management team brings over 20 years of care experience, working in partnership with residents, families, social workers and multidisciplinary specialists.

  • Certified manager, resident nurse & qualified care assistants
  • 24/7 uniformed security with CCTV — fully gated premises
  • Person-centred, dementia-friendly care environment
  • Mini-farm, therapy garden & recreational gazebo
  • Open family visits — all day, no appointments needed

How Can We Help You?

Submit an enquiry, book an assessment, schedule a doctor's appointment, apply to visit a resident, or make a donation — all in one place.

Submit a Care Enquiry

Tell us about your loved one's needs and we will get back to you within 24 hours with personalised information and next steps.

💬
What Happens Next?
  • Our admissions team reviews your enquiry within 24 hours
  • We call you to discuss your loved one's needs in detail
  • A free home or care home visit is arranged
  • A personalised care quote is prepared for you
  • We guide you through funding options (including government support)
Call us directly: 0814 636 0636 / 070 51 51 51 05 — our friendly team is available 7 days a week, 8am–8pm.
Request a Care Assessment

A comprehensive care needs assessment is the first step. Our qualified assessor will evaluate your loved one's physical, emotional and social needs to create a tailored care plan.

📋
About Our Assessment
  • Conducted by our certified care manager or senior nurse
  • Covers physical health, mental health, mobility and daily living
  • Takes approximately 1–2 hours at a time convenient for you
  • Completely free of charge and without obligation
  • A detailed written care plan is provided within 5 working days
  • Assessment informs care type recommendation and fee estimate
Please bring: GP referral letter (if available), list of current medications, and any previous health assessments.
Book a Doctor's Appointment

Schedule a medical consultation for a current resident or for a prospective resident prior to admission. Our visiting doctor holds regular clinics at Hope Villa.

🩺
Our Medical Support
  • Visiting GP holds clinics at Hope Villa twice weekly
  • Resident nurse coordinates all appointments and records
  • Specialist referrals facilitated to hospitals in Ibadan
  • Medication reviews conducted monthly for all residents
  • Emergency medical referrals actioned within hours
  • Family members informed of all medical updates
Medical emergencies: Contact our nurses directly on 0814 636 0636. For life-threatening emergencies, call the nearest hospital immediately.
Apply to Visit a Resident

Family are always welcome at BunnyCare. While no appointment is generally needed, filling in this form helps us prepare a warm welcome and ensure mealtimes are respected.

🏡
Visiting Guidelines
  • Visits are welcome any day between 9am – 6pm
  • Please avoid mealtimes: 8–9am, 1–2pm, and 6–7pm
  • All visitors must sign in at the reception on arrival
  • Children are welcome and bring great joy to our residents
  • Day outings can be arranged with 48hrs notice
  • Overnight stays are not permitted within the facility
First time visiting? Please bring a valid ID. Our staff will give you a warm welcome tour of the home and introduce you to our care team.
